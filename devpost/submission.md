# AI Life Navigator — Devpost Submission

## Inspiration
People struggle with consistency, motivation, and understanding their own behavior.  
AI Life Navigator solves this by combining habit tracking, mood logging, and AI‑powered insights.

## What It Does
- Generates personalized daily plans
- Tracks habits and streaks
- Logs mood and stress
- Detects global patterns
- Provides AI coaching

## How We Built It
- MeDo UI builder
- MeDo Workflow Engine
- MeDo Database
- AI Coach integration
- JSON‑based data structures
